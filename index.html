Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What core mechanical design services does Susilya provide?

Susilya provides comprehensive mechanical design services, including turnkey product development, custom machine design, advanced 3D CAD modeling, and precision 2D drawings. We specialize in structural FEM analysis and the exact component selection of linear guideways, ball screws, servo motors, and pneumatic systems for production-ready blueprints.

Do you offer custom 3D CAD modeling and legacy conversion?

Yes. We deliver highly accurate 3D CAD models for machine parts, assemblies, architectural structures, and process plants. These models optimize physical project erection, weldment/casting creation, and future R&D. We also convert legacy paper drawings into production-ready, digital 2D detailing blueprints for efficient fabrication.

What is included in Susilya’s machine design training courses?

Our industry-certified machine design training courses offer project-based learning for students and engineering professionals. The curriculum features masterclasses in leading 3D CAD software and corporate expert-led modules on GD&T (Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing), bridging the gap between classroom theory and real-world manufacturing applications.

Can you create engineering content for marketing and product promotions?

Yes. Beyond core engineering, we create high-quality promotional and advertising content. This includes realistic product photos featuring your company logo, professional catalogs, and engaging product demo animated videos designed to effectively market, advertise, and demonstrate your mechanical products to potential clients.
