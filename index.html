Custom Mechanical Design & CAD Outsourcing Services

Our engineering team specializes in advanced 3D CAD modeling, structural analysis, industrial automation, and legacy drawing conversion. Through our mechanical design services, we deliver production-ready blueprints, manufacturing data, and prototypes tailored to your operational constraints.

What We Provide

Design and Selection of Parts, Assemblies & Structures

We provide Creation of Ultimate Machine Body Structure and parts using FEM Analysis. We select proper Machine Components such as Linear Guideways, Ball Screws, Servo Motors, Precision Gearboxes, Hydraulic/Pneumatic Cylinders, Bearings, Chucks, O rings/Quadrings, Seals etc which are highly suitable to your products and Structures.

3D CAD Models

We provide Exact 3D Models of Machine Parts & Aseemblies, Steel & Architectural Structures and Process Plants which are highly useful in physical erection of projects, for creating weldment/casting and for further Research and Development.

2D Drawings & Detailings

We provide part drawings, assembly drawings, layout drawings, steel erection drawings for successful and effecient fabrication, assembly and erection of your projects.

Promotional and Advertising Contents
