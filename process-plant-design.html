Frequently Asked Questions

What types of process plants do you design?

Susilya provides comprehensive process design, piping engineering, and equipment detailing solutions across diverse industrial sectors. We specialize in custom infrastructure layouts for oil and gas refineries, petrochemical facilities, chemical processing operations, and industrial water or wastewater treatment plants.

Do you generate and digitize PFDs and P&IDs?

Yes. We specialize in generating, modifying, and intelligently digitizing Process Flow Diagrams (PFD) and Piping & Instrumentation Diagrams (P&ID). Our team accurately translates raw concepts into industry-standard schematics required for plant evaluation, construction, and asset operations management.

What deliverables do you provide for industrial piping engineering?

We supply production-ready deliverables including General Arrangement (GA) layouts, equipment placement footprints, and detailed piping isometric drawings. Each package contains explicit isometric spool data, component weld maps, and highly accurate material take-offs (MTO) with comprehensive bills of materials (BOM).

Can you manage piping stress analysis and pipe support design?

Yes. We execute static and dynamic piping stress analysis to assess thermal expansion, optimize support layouts, and run flange leakage checks. Our engineers design standard, variable spring, rigid, and specialized structural pipe rack support systems to preserve structural plant integrity.

Do you convert 3D laser scans or hand sketches into CAD?

Yes. We handle as-built plant modeling by converting 3D point cloud laser scan data into intelligent CAD models. We also migrate legacy 2D blueprints, PDF schematics, and hand-drawn rough sketches into accurate piping documents and modular skid-mounted process designs.
