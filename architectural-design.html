Frequently Asked Questions

What types of building projects do your architectural services cover?

Susilya delivers complete architectural planning, concept generation, and detailing solutions. Our project portfolio spans across diverse real estate sectors, including custom residential villas, modern apartments, commercial office spaces, multi-use retail complex buildings, distribution warehouses, and large-scale industrial manufacturing facilities.

Do you provide high-fidelity 3D BIM modelling and coordination?

Yes. We specialize in advanced 3D BIM modelling for precise spatial coordination, parametric component design, and proactive collision detection. This structural approach minimizes field errors, clarifies spatial relationships, and ensures accurate material estimation data before construction begins.

What drafting and construction documentation do you deliver?

We generate comprehensive Construction Documentation (CD Sets) and precise working drawings for site contractors. Our deliverables include highly detailed floor plans, site boundaries, exterior building elevations, cross-sections, interior reflected ceiling plans (RCP), flooring layouts, and custom millwork joinery details.

Can you create realistic 3D renderings and walkthroughs?

Yes. We produce high-quality, realistic rendered photos and interactive walkthrough animated videos of interior and exterior spaces. These photorealistic assets are optimized for high-impact client presentations, design approval workflows, pre-construction marketing campaigns, and commercial project catalogs.

How do you handle point cloud data or hand sketches?

We provide accurate as-built modelling from laser scans, point cloud data, or on-site physical measurements. Our engineering drafting team quickly converts static legacy blueprints, PDF plan sheets, and hand-drawn rough sketches into editable layouts or intelligent models.
